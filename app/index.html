What is a foodcoop?

A co-op is a collective where everyone shares the workload for the benefit of the whole. A food co-op is a group of people that buy food from suppliers of their own choosing. A collective do-it-yourself supermarket.

Join a Work Group

A work group is a group of members who work together and are responsible for a specific need of our food co-op. Some examples of work groups are administration, membership, orders, financial, etc. As a member of our food co-op, you have to join a work group.
